Why This Book Exists

Every year, hundreds of millions in royalties go uncollected

Not stolen — uncollected. The money sits in accounts waiting for artists who never showed up to claim it.

The music industry runs on a simple bet: that you won't understand what you own, how it makes money, or how to collect it. This book is the other side of that bet.
